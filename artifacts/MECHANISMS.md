# Mechanism Map

**Signal:** Amazon Bedrock multi-agent collaboration reaches GA
- **Mechanism 1: Workflow Control Plane** - Execution shifts from isolated prompts to policy-governed multi-agent orchestration.
- **Mechanism 2: Coordination** - Human oversight becomes checkpoint-based rather than step-by-step manual execution.
- **Mechanism 3: Verification & Audit** - Multi-agent outcomes require more granular provenance to adjudicate failures.

**Signal:** OpenAI introduces ChatGPT agent
- **Mechanism 1: Identity/Accountability** - Delegated actions require explicit authorization and ownership mapping.
- **Mechanism 2: Trust Infrastructure** - Trust moves from model output quality to constrained action reliability.
- **Mechanism 3: Pricing/Budgeting** - Teams must budget for governance overhead as delegated execution grows.

**Signal:** OpenAI Operator integrated into ChatGPT agent mode
- **Mechanism 1: Verification & Audit** - Browser interactions require machine-readable evidence trails.
- **Mechanism 2: Procurement** - Enterprise buying shifts toward platforms with enforceable controls and observability.
- **Mechanism 3: Market Design** - Assistant, operator, and orchestration capabilities converge under unified control frameworks.
