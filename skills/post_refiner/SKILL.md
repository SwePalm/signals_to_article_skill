# Post Refiner

**Goal:** Polish the stone.

## Instructions
1.  **Input:**
    *   `artifacts/DRAFT_POST.md`
    *   `artifacts/CRITIQUES.md`

2.  **Action:** Rewrite the draft to address the valid critiques.

    *   Preserve the mandatory specific structure and disclaimer.

## Deliverable
